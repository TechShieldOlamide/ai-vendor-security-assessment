# AI Vendor Security Assessment Toolkit

A practical questionnaire and assessment framework for evaluating AI tools and vendors before adoption, covering data handling, model governance, security, and compliance with the NDPA 2023 and emerging AI regulations.

## Why This Exists

AI adoption is outpacing AI governance in most businesses. Teams sign up for AI tools, paste customer data into chatbots, and integrate third-party AI APIs, often without anyone in compliance or security reviewing what happens to that data. This toolkit gives organisations a structured way to ask the right questions before adopting an AI tool, not after something goes wrong.

## Who This Is For

- Compliance and GRC teams reviewing AI tools before procurement
- Vendor risk management teams adding AI-specific questions to existing assessments
- Founders and product teams integrating third-party AI APIs
- Consultants advising clients on AI governance and vendor risk

## The Core Problem

Most vendor security questionnaires were written before AI existed. They ask about encryption, access controls, and incident response, all still relevant, but they don't ask the AI-specific questions that actually matter now: does this tool train on our data, who has access to our prompts, can we get meaningful human review of automated decisions.

This toolkit closes that gap.
