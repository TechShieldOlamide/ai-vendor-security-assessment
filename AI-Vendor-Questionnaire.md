## Red Flags to Watch For

- Vague or evasive answers about whether customer data trains models
- No willingness to sign a Data Processing Agreement
- No documented incident response process
- Inability to explain, even at a high level, how the AI reaches decisions
- Refusal to disclose sub-processors or underlying model providers
