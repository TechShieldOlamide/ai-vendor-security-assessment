# Regulatory Alignment Checklist for AI Vendors

**Purpose:** Mapping AI vendor use to the NDPA 2023 and emerging AI-specific regulation, so adoption decisions account for compliance obligations, not just functionality.
**Prepared by:** TrustGrid Technology

---

## Why This Matters

Adopting an AI tool is not just a procurement decision, it is a compliance decision. The NDPA 2023 already governs automated decision-making and data processing, regardless of whether the processing happens through your own systems or a third-party AI vendor.

---

## NDPA 2023 Alignment

| NDPA Requirement | AI Vendor Question to Ask |
|---|---|
| Section 25: Lawful basis for processing | What lawful basis applies to data sent through this AI tool, and has it been documented? |
| Section 27: Transparency about automated decision-making | If this tool influences decisions about individuals, has this been disclosed in your privacy notice? |
| Section 37: Right not to be subject to solely automated decisions | Does this tool make decisions with legal or significant effects without human review? |
| Data Protection Impact Assessment | Has a DPIA been completed for this specific AI use case, particularly if special category data is involved? |
| Cross-border transfer safeguards | If the vendor processes data outside Nigeria, are appropriate transfer safeguards in place? |

---

## EU AI Act Relevance (If Applicable)

If your organisation serves EU customers or the AI tool affects EU residents, additional considerations apply:

| Question | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| Does this AI use case fall into a high-risk category under the EU AI Act (e.g. credit scoring, employment screening)? | High-risk systems face significantly stricter obligations |
| Has the vendor provided documentation supporting EU AI Act compliance for their model? | Vendors serving EU markets should have this available |
| Is the AI system used to make automated decisions affecting EU residents without meaningful human review? | This may violate both the EU AI Act and GDPR Article 22 |

## International Standards Alignment

| Standard | What to Check |
|---|---|
| ISO/IEC 42001 | Does the vendor hold or reference alignment with this AI management system standard? |
| NIST AI Risk Management Framework | Has the vendor published any alignment with the Govern, Map, Measure, Manage functions? |

---

## Documentation to Request From Vendors

Before finalising adoption of any AI tool processing sensitive data, request:

- A copy of their Data Processing Agreement
- Their data retention and deletion policy
- Their model training data policy (see Model-Training-and-Data-Use.md)
- Evidence of any relevant certifications (ISO 27001, ISO 42001, SOC 2)
- Their incident response and breach notification process
- Documentation supporting any claims of regulatory alignment
